About Us

The nonprofit chosen by our website, Rescuing Dogs, helps rescue abused, neglected and careless dogs. It informs people on good pet ownership and how it's much cheaper to adopt instead of buy.

It would also make it much more visible because most of the foundations that have similar goal to ours will often provide us an easy way to report to them through their Facebook page and it can open up options for foundations with similar goals to us to develop a website where consumers can report or bring their pets to us if they are determined to be incapable of caring for them.

It aims to create a website where people can put up information use that information. It aims to use the website where people can share information to potential dogs that need an actual home where they can be properly taken care of and use that information to find out local government to cooperate with them to rescue those dogs instead of euthanizing them.

It aims to use the website as both rescuing website and adoption site where people can choose to adopt specific dogs, but they need to prove that they can take care of these dogs so that they won't have to experience the abandonment they once experienced and be able to use the site to check up on them and see how those dogs are doing.
